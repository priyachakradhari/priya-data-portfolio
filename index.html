About Me

Professional Background

My journey into data analytics started with a simple question: "What story is hidden in this spreadsheet?" That curiosity has taken me from analyzing sales patterns in Excel to building end-to-end data pipelines in Azure. Currently at Krishnav Tech, I'm working with SQL Server, PySpark, and Azure Databricks—turning raw data into insights that actually matter. Before this, I spent two months at ELEVATE LABS learning the ropes of real-time data visualization, which taught me that a well-designed dashboard can speak louder than a hundred-page report. I've completed job simulations with Deloitte and Goldman Sachs, earning certifications from platforms like HackerRank and Kaggle along the way. What drives me isn't just the tech—it's the "aha" moment when stakeholders see patterns they never noticed before.

Technical Expertise

I'm fluent in Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, Matplotlib), SQL, Power BI, Tableau, and advanced Excel—basically, if it involves data, I've probably built something with it. My sweet spot? Taking messy datasets and transforming them into interactive dashboards that decision-makers can actually use. I know my way around dimensional modeling (Star and Snowflake schemas, fact and dimension tables, SCD types), and I've designed ERDs and databases that hold up in production. Recently, I've been exploring AI workflow automation with n8n and Make, because who doesn't love watching repetitive tasks handle themselves? Whether it's writing complex SQL queries with CTEs and window functions, creating predictive models in Python, or building ETL pipelines in Azure Data Factory, I believe the best technical skills are the ones that solve real problems—not just the ones that look good on a resume.
